Privacy Policy for Konfuzio Tools

Last updated: August 11, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Konfuzio Tools is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our PDF processing services:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our services
  • Technical Data: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security purposes
  • Document Processing: Your documents are processed locally and are not stored on our servers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our PDF processing services
  • Improve and optimize our tools
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All document processing is done locally on your device
  • No documents are stored on our servers
  • Encrypted transmission for any data that must be transmitted
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for essential functionality:

  • Session cookies for maintaining your preferences
  • Analytics cookies (optional) to improve our services
  • No third-party tracking cookies

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics (Google Analytics, if enabled)
  • Error reporting and monitoring
  • All third-party services are carefully selected and comply with privacy regulations

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary:

  • Usage data: Up to 2 years
  • Technical logs: Up to 90 days
  • Documents: Not stored on our servers

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside the European Economic Area (EEA). We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
